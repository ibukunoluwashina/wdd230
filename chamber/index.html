News Release

Akure Chamber of Commerce is pleased to announce that we have entered into a definitive merger agreement with Renewable Energy Network Solution, a leading provider of Solar Powered Energy in Nigeria. This merger with Renewable Energy Network Solution, based in Akure, Nigeria, further strengthens our position in the Renewable Energy market. The combination of Renewable Energy Network solution's power distribution with our group of partners will provide our clients with cheap electricity supply. This partnership is also expected to result in greater efficiencies and significantly increase our market share. As with all important business decisions, we will work closely with our clients, partners and employees to make the integration process as smooth as possible. The integration of both companies will take place over the next few month. Of course, in the meantime our customers will continue to receive the same high quality electric power supply, which they have come to expect.
